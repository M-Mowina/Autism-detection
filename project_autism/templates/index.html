Autism

Autism spectrum disorder (ASD) is a complicated neurological developmental disorder that manifests itself in a variety of ways. The child diagnosed with ASD and their parents’ daily lives can be dramatically improved with early diagnosis and appropriate medical intervention. According to the World Health Organization in 2014, it confirmed that the number of people with autism in Egypt is estimated at about 800,000. At the same time, the Children’s Department of the General Secretariat of Mental Health announced that the prevalence of autism in Egypt reached 1%. In 2017 the Minister of Health confirmed Social Solidarity This number indicates that there is one out of every 160 children infected with different degrees of symptoms. The reason for choosing this project lies in the early diagnosis of children with autism spectrum disorder, which is difficult to diagnose early by specialists at young ages. The difficulty of detecting autism in its early stages lies in the lack of any biological symptoms that can be used in diagnosis.
